AI vs IT: The Battle Behind the Screen

Replacement?

Behind the screens of many, a hidden conflict is taking place. With AI being used more and more recently, IT professionals have voiced their concerns. From companies choosing to replace personnel with AI, many are displeased and anxious about the future. Just how much and how far will AI be used to try to replace us? This blog aims to shed light and break down the details to this rising issue.



Anxiety Creeps In

Even before OpenAI released ChatGPT, AI were already present but they were not as smart or interactive as today. From being simple chatbots that we once used in our childhood, it has now evolved to a level where we ask it for help in all fields. When I was young, we had a programming lesson. At the time, it felt so amazing that it was possible to communicate and manipulate the computer using programming languages. But now, even without learning those specialized languages, we are now able to communicate directly. Here is where the anxiety comes in, as a student taking bachelor's in computer science, it has been a real worry that after graduating, we wouldn't see any purpose or task for us. Hearing that many IT specialists are being replaced by AI makes it feel like technology is leaving us behind when we were supposed to be the one exploring and improving it. In a world where everything seems to move so fast and stopping is a ticket to obselence, what if you're already running in the direction you thought to be, but suddenly the road you're stepping on is going faster than you. It gives a sense of meaninglessness and purposelessness, that your hard work for years is just quickly being created by something not even another human.



The Rat Race

With that negative statement, it is clear that AI has a massive impact of all of us. Some positive, some negative, but to others much more negative. In this situation, improving and striving is the only choice. It has become a rat race to see who would come out on top first. But to that one success, many more are left behind in the dark. Lost and afraid with no end in sight. Forever struggling, wondering if they made the right choice in their life.
