Ireland

Why Here?

One of my friend's sister is a nurse working here. She said that the weather here is very calm and cool. The air is clean and the environment is very calming.





Castles

I'm very interested in historical sites, especially castles. I find them very interesting and a sight to see. Something old and been attacked many times, but still standing strong today. A blast from the past.





Culture

The Irish culture is very spread out in the world. The 4 leaf clover is a symbol of luck, even I use a keychain of it for luck. Saint Patrick's day is something I have seen in many games. The characters and the accent is very iconic too.





Beer

Even if I am not into alcohol, I am still curious of the beer made in Ireland. Stereotypical as it may sound, I have heard many good things about the beer in Ireland and that its process method makes it better to ingest than other alcohol.





Cliffs

First of all, it is not for jumping. The cliffs here just look so amazing. It looks like the end of the world. It looks just like a sudden drop. It is so flat too.
