Can you Fix my Washing Machine?

Sir Engineer?

Ilang beses na akong nasabihan ng IT ka diba? Ayusin mo nga yung electric fan. O kaya habang nagdidikit Ng microphone para sa karaoke Dapat Ikaw gumagawa nito eh. I always reply back, Engineering Naman Yan, di kami ang gumagawa niyan. Like, how is it na sobrang different ng expectations sa atin from what I actually should do? It makes me wonder, after I graduate, will I design and develop systems or will I have to become a pseudo-engineer?



Hot and Cold, Soft and Hard

Since almost halfway through na ako sa course ko, I think it's time to start thinking about ano talagang gagawin ko sa future. I always envisioned it na possibly nasa cubicle ako, sa harap Ng computer nagcocode or gumagamit Ng Microsoft tools. Or nasa Isang group meeting, Anong features ang maganda ilagay at paano ayusin ang bugs. Yung mga ibang Kilala Kong CS graduates tumulong sa development Ng GCash, PayMaya, mga app development. Pero yung iba din Naman sinabi na parang more on hardware Sila na parang naging computer or electronics engineering na sila. Dahil don, thinking of my future career, anong skills ba talaga Ang kailangan ko i-develop? For this, I think it's important to distinguish each pros and cons and figure out kung ano ba talaga ang reason ko dito. So, sa hardware, syempre nandiyan yung mga circuits, microchips, semiconductors, etc. Ang kinagandahan Naman dito ay maraming taong need Ng maalam sa hardware kasi delicate ito. Ang problema nga lang is pwede ka mareplace Ng mga automatic manufacturing. Sa software Naman, ginagamit ito Ng lahat, from simple apps, to webpages, to operating systems. But, there is this problem with AI. So ano ba ang habol ko at nandito ako sa field na ito, truth be told, pera. Money, of course, who wouldn't want to live comfortably and be able to do what you want. To achieve that goal, I think it's necessary not pick pick and choose, but to improve and learn. Hindi naman ito something na pag pinili ang isa, locked out na yung other. Why pick, when I can do both. It's much better sa resume and sa life skills. Doing both allows me to be more secure and useful sa trabaho. Pag alam mo paano nag o-operate ang dalawang bagay, you would be able to easily integrate both of these to create a symbiotic synergy. Thus, a solid foundation is needed. Building up knowledge in both fields by attending seminars, acquiring certificates, and going into workshops para tunay na mahasa ang sarili.



Skill Check

So, to pick between software or hardware, I choose both. Both because they are two sides of the same coin, they complement each other. Nagbibigay ito Ng flexibility and space para makamit ko ang goal Kong mabuhay comfortably. And to gain the necessary skills for that, I would expand my scopes and experiences, find new paths, and hone myself in knowledge.
