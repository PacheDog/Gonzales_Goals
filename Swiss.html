Swiss Alps

Why Here?

The Swiss Alps is in the country of Switzerland. This country is famous for its neutrality for over 300 years. Therefore, this place is tranquil and peaceful.





Skiing

Since the Philippines doesn't have any snowy peaks, skiing can only be done in others. The snowy mountains of the Swiss Alps look so fluffy so I want to try skiing down there, even if it will most likely end in a fall.





Swiss Cheese

I have been hearing many good things about the cheese fondue of Switzerland. Many people have been showing dipping many things into this melty cheese and it looks delicious. So, I want to visit this place to taste this creamy delicacy.





Hiking

The Swiss Alps are one of the most stunning places to visit. From pictures alone, the sight is beautiful. I want to see and smell the clean nature to see if it looks and smells as it is beautiful in pictures.





Swiss Chocolate

With famous chocolate brands such as Toblerone, Switzerland is a must visit for those with a sweet tooth. I want to see the peak represented in the Toblerone and the many other delicious chocolates that the country is known for.
