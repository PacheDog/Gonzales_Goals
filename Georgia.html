Georgia

Why Here?

The snow looks nice. Also, I have been watching a Georgian Youtuber (Gattsu) and he has been telling about the way things are there. My friend's sister has also been there and it looks nice.





Skiing

Georgia has the Caucasus Mountains. These are big mountains that skiing is often done on them on snowy times. Other than skiing, I really want to experience the snow here since my friend's sister talked about it.





Hiking

The mountains look beautiful, even if the trek might be difficult. The view will be very amazing. Other than the act of hiking the mountain, I also want to see how far I would reach with the cold climate.





Historical Sites

Georgia has been mentioned in many history books. In recent times during the time of World War 2, this place played a key role in the fighting. Commie Blocks are also commonly mentioned so I want to see these too. There are also cathedrals





Food

Because of Gattsu, the youtuber, has been praising their food many times and comparing them with other countries' food, I want to test this for myself and judge the taste of their cuisine.
