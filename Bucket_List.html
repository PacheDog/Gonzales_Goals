1. Spend One Million in One Day

I would like to be able to spend One Million Pesos in One Day. This would be a significant milestone since this would mean that I have reached the level that I can spend 1 million without worrying.

Status: Not Completed





2. Live Healthily to 60 Years

Just living to 60 years old is not the goal, but the healthily part. Being of 60 years but can still be of functioning level is my goal. This would mean that I took care of myself that in that late stage, I can still function on my own. Furthermore, it would also help be enjoy old age.

Status: Ongoing





3. Buy a House in Tagaytay or Baguio

This is sort of a way for me to repay my parents. They have always talked about living in colder areas, especially during summer. So giving them a summer house for summers would be nice.

Status: Not Completed





4. Learn How to Cook

I can cook rice, sinigang, adobo, any others. But, I want to learn the other cooking methods that I see in the Internet, dishes that look made by a professional. I don't plan on going into Culinary but just being good enough at cooking that my loved ones can enjoy them.

Status: Ongoing





5. Retire at 50

If possible, I would like to retire at 50 or something around that age. Its so that I can be able to enjoy life while sting being able to move without struggling. I don't want to just work until I die.

Status: Not Completed





6. Graduate

I want to graduate. Graduating with honors and achievements would be nice, but nobody is putting pressure on me for that. This would help me in my future with financial security. But this is also something for myself, something I can be proud of.

Status: Ongoing





7. A Loving Family

When or if I get a family of my own, I want to be able to provide them with comfort and care. That they don't need to worry about expenses. That when they are in trouble, they don't fear asking for help from me. That they don't feel they aren't loved.

Status: Not Completed





8. Own a Kubo

I really want a kubo. Its simple and cool but also home-y. It can be used for hanging outside but with the comfort of being inside. Its open air so probably not near the city so that the air is fresh.

Status: Not Completed





9. Own a High-end Computer

I want a computer that can run any task I want it without issue. This would contain PC parts that are the best of the best. That when running, it would feel very smooth and very fast.

Status: Not Completed





10. Eat All Flavors of Buldak

I want to eat and taste all flavors of the Buldak. From the least spicy to the most. From experience, this actually increases my tolerance of spices. Also, if possible, mix all the flavors into one and eat that.

Status: Ongoing
