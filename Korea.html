Korea

Why Here?

Korea, specifically South Korea, has been very influential of recent times. From songs to films, I have seen many things about Korea and I want to see and experience it personally.





Food

The samgyup is a korean influence here that has become a massive hit. For celebrations, samgyup has become the way to go. This country has also introduced Kimchi to us. So, I want to see the different foods that became this influence.





K-pop

You can't talk about Korea without mentioning one of their biggest export, K-pop. From Gangnam style to BTS, this has brought many attention to k-pop. To see and experience the concerts of idols up close would be amazing.





Culture

Since the introduction of Korean films in our screens, I have witnessed many Korean customs and tradtions. From their clothing to their mannerisms, seeing this up close is one of the reasons why I want to visit this place.





Historical Sites

I am a fan of history. There are many places in Korea that is mentioned in books and games. Not only the stories behind these places but also the aesthetics of the buildings.
