Japan

Why Here?

Japan is something that has influenced me greatly. I have always been a fan of anime since I was young. This country has also been mentioned many times in history books.





Anime

Japan is where anime was born. Not only anime, manga was also born here. I have been watching anime and reading manga for years now. This place is where those anime and mangas were created so I want to visit here.





Mt. Fuji

Mt. Fuji is very iconic to Japan. It has a very unique appearance that one can tell from just looking at it. It has also been used in movies many times. Therefore, I want to see its majesty from a close view.





Culture

Japanese culture is very influential all around the world. It is very iconic, from the pink cherry blossoms, Ramen bowls, to kimonos. Trying this out personally has been a long time goal of mine.





Historical Sites

Japan is home to many events that has impacted the world. The Meiji Restoration opened up the country to the outside. World War 2 included the Philippines and Japan. I want to the see history of these places to learn more about the past.
